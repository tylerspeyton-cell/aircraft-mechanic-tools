🧮 Key Conversion Formulas

Temperature

°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
ISA standard day: 59°F (15°C) at sea level

Torque

N·m = ft·lb × 1.35582
ft·lb = in·lb ÷ 12
Always verify torque spec from AMM

Pressure

bar = PSI × 0.0689476
kPa = PSI × 6.89476
1 atm = 14.696 PSI = 1.01325 bar = 101.325 kPa

Length

mm = inches × 25.4
meters = feet × 0.3048
1" = 25.4 mm exactly

Weight

kg = lb × 0.453592
lb = kg × 2.20462
Fuel: Jet-A ≈ 6.7 lb/US gal (varies with temperature)

Volume / Fuel

liters = US gal × 3.78541
US gal = liters × 0.26417
Avgas ≈ 6.0 lb/US gal | Jet-A ≈ 6.7 lb/US gal

Torque Wrench Extension

TW = (TS × L) ÷ (L + E)
TW = wrench reading | TS = target torque | L = wrench effective length | E = extension length. Measure both from the drive centerline.

Rivet Hole Diameter

Hole = rivet dia. + 0.002" to 0.004"
Per AC 43.13. Minimum edge distance = 2× rivet diameter. Minimum pitch = 3× rivet diameter.
